Honey Garlic Chicken with Rosemary

What To Do

This simple-yet-impressive dinner is on the table in under an hour. The moist chicken is easy to prep with just a few ingredients you probably already have on hand, like garlic powder and honey.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broccolini
  • Lemon Pesto Pasta
  • Cilantro Rice
    Step 1

    Season Chicken fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.

    Step 2

    Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil together in a small bowl until sugar is dissolved.
    Place Chicken in a large resealable plastic bag; add soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

    Step 3

    Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.

    Step 4

    Place Chicken on the preheated grill, and discard marinade.
    Cook Chicken until 200 degrees internal. about 15 to 20 minutes per side.
